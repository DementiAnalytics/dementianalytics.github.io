About us

Our technology gathers real-time data to aid healthcare professionals in diagnosing Alzheimer’s disease and other dementia-related disorders. This technology utilizes AI to provide an accurate, and non-invasive solution for the early detection and real-time monitoring of Alzheimer’s. Harnessing AI, the platform instantly analyzes speech to deliver a real-time, probability-based dementia diagnosis. Its innovative monitoring feature continually tracks a subject’s cognitive status
