DementiAnalytics

Early dementia detection

Our technology gathers real-time data to aid healthcare professionals in diagnosing Alzheimer’s disease and other dementia-related disorders. This technology utilizes AI to provide an accurate, and non-invasive solution for the early detection and real-time monitoring of Alzheimer’s Disease. Harnessing AI, the platform instantly analyzes speech to deliver a real-time, probability-based dementia diagnosis. Its innovative monitoring feature continually tracks a subject’s cognitive status

About us

Formed by a shared vision of a future where early dementia diagnosis is swift and effective, we are a group of dedicated researchers transitioning from academia to entrepreneurship. Leveraging our combined expertise in applied AI, we're developing cutting-edge technology to address the challenge of early dementia detection with a human-centered approach. Join us as we strive to make a real difference in the lives of patients and their families.
